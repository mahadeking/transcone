Questions & answers

Everything you might wonder about converting with Transcone.

Are my images uploaded anywhere?

No. Transcone converts everything locally in your browser using your device's own processing power. Your files never leave your computer, so nothing can be stored, shared, or leaked.

Does it work on large or high-resolution images?

Because everything runs on your device, performance depends on your device's memory rather than a server. Small icons and large high-resolution photos are both handled locally.

Which formats can I convert?

Input: JPG, PNG, WebP, AVIF, GIF, BMP, SVG, HEIC/HEIF, TIFF and PDF. Output: JPG, PNG, WebP, AVIF, GIF, BMP, TIFF, SVG, PDF and ICO.

Does it work offline?

Once the page has loaded, standard formats convert fully offline. A few formats (HEIC, TIFF, PDF) use small helper libraries that load once from the web.

Will my images lose quality?

For lossless formats (PNG, TIFF, BMP) quality is preserved exactly. For lossy formats (JPG, WebP, AVIF) you control the quality slider — keep it high for near-original results, or lower it for smaller files.

Can I use it for commercial work?

Yes — Transcone works for both personal and commercial projects: convert client assets, product images, or anything else.
